Who We Are

A North Carolina where women are connected, supported, and inspired to lead.

She Leads NC brings women together through storytelling, leadership, service, and community — creating opportunities for women from all backgrounds to connect, learn from one another, and make a meaningful impact.

We were created to bring those voices together.

Our Vision

A North Carolina where women are connected, supported, and inspired to lead, serve, and strengthen their communities.

Our Mission

Bringing women together through storytelling, leadership, service, and community — creating opportunities for every woman to thrive.

Leadership Exists Everywhere.

From boardrooms to kitchen tables, from town halls to volunteer drives — leadership lives wherever women choose to show up for one another.

In Schools

Educators shaping the next generation of leaders, learners, and changemakers.

In Churches

Women of faith serving congregations, communities, and one another with quiet power.

In Community Groups

Nonprofit leaders, neighborhood organizers, and volunteers strengthening the fabric of NC.

In Government

From town councils to the state capitol — women shaping policy, place, and possibility.

our four pillars

How She Leads NC Shows Up.

Four guiding pillars shape everything we do — and every woman who steps into our circle.

01

Storytelling

Stories connect us, teach us, and preserve wisdom. Through panels, interviews, and events, women share lessons learned and experiences that shaped them.

02

Leadership

Leadership is not defined by a title. We celebrate it wherever it exists — in boardrooms, classrooms, faith communities, and around kitchen tables.

03

Service

Food drives, hygiene drives, professional clothing, women's reentry support, and family assistance — meeting practical needs while creating pathways.

04

Community

From the mountains to the coast — building authentic relationships across professions, generations, experiences, and backgrounds.
